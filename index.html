BUBBLE BURST

INSTRUCTIONS:

  • Moving bubbles of random size gets generated anywhere on the screen
  • Click anywhere on the bubble to burst it
  • You are awarded 1 point for bursting a bubble and 5 points for bursting ROCK BUBBLE(needs 5 clicks)
  • The rate of bubble generation increases with time
  • Slow the generation by using FELIX FELICES which can be used for 2 times
  • Use Gauntlets,that appear rarely, to clear half of the bubbles
  • When more than 75% of screen gets filled with bubbles for more than 10 seconds, the game ends
  • Be quick and burst as fast as you can to keep playing!
